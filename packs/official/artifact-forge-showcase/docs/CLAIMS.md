# Claims and non-claims

Every part in this pack is a validator-backed *geometry* promise — and
nothing more. The pack explicitly does NOT claim:

- **Not pressure-rated.** Hose adapters are for low-risk workshop and
  household lines (vacuum hoses, drain hoses, garden watering). Never use
  them on pressurized, potable-water or heated lines.
- **Not potable-water certified. Not food-safe.** FDM prints are porous
  and the materials are not certified for food or drinking-water contact.
- **Not a certified measuring tool.** Jigs and gauges are shop probes;
  verify the first article. A tolerance ladder teaches your printer's
  bands — it is not a calibrated instrument.
- **Not OEM equivalence.** A printed spare is derived from your
  measurements; strength and lifespan differ from the molded original.
  Verify fit before use.
- **Not medical, not electrical-certified, not child-certified (EN71),
  not load-bearing beyond desktop use.**

Every report carries the relevant note automatically; removing the
wording does not remove the physics.
